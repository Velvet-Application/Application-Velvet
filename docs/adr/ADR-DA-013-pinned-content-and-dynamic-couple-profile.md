# ADR-DA-013 – Dynamic Couple Profiles & Pinned Content

Status: Accepted

## Decisions

- Couple profiles are composed of three linked identities: Couple, Partner A and Partner B.
- Each individual identity can be independently activated or hidden.
- Each identity has its own preferences, visibility, searches, albums, availability and presentation.
- The shared couple profile remains the common entry point and history.
- Profiles can pin important content at the top of the profile (photo, video, text, current search, event, trip or announcement).
- Pinned content can be reordered and optionally expire automatically.

## UX principles

- One premium card design adapted to each profile type.
- Couple cards prominently display the shared photo plus two individual portraits.
- No visual hierarchy based on popularity or seniority.
- Optional presentation videos complement photos.
